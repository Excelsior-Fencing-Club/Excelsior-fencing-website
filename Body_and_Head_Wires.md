Memo from the Board of Directors

We would like to kindly inform all members that, starting in September, the club will no longer be able to provide body or head wires for borrowing.

Over time, a number of wires have gone missing, and unfortunately this is no longer sustainable for the club. As a result, we encourage all fencers to please purchase their own equipment before this time to ensure a smooth transition.

Thank you for your understanding and continued support.

Board of Directors.

Excelsior Fencing Club.
